- We are creating a personal website for John Dengis.
- John Dengis is a software engineer by trade and spent the better part of 7 years as an engineering leader at Instacart.
- John has also made YouTube videos previously at the following channel https://www.youtube.com/@JohnDengisYT. These were primarily cooking, culture and travel videos.
- John also likes making music was in a few bands in the past, including a progressive metal band. John still likes to play guitar and tinker with gear and signal chains. I can include pictures and descriptions of all of my gear and the reasons for choosing them in a section on the web page.
- John learned Chinese before, reached roughly B2 level (HSK 4) but is kind of rusty. John is currently learning Japanese and is around N4 - N3 level and is practicing every day.
- John loves animals and has a dog and a cat.

## Task

- We will create a unique and personalized website for John Dengis
- The expected domain for this website will be https://john.deng.is/

## Requirements

- Reusable component elements of the design should be but in ./app/components/ui/ folder. This is things like reusable buttons, cards, fields, toggles etc.
- Website should support both dark mode and light mode.
- The website should have an index page with general overview, a more detailed "about" page, a list of projects referring to specific projects that John has made, such as his GitHub repos, YouTube etc., a blog with RSS feed. The blog posts should have hero images at the top and there should be squared thumbnails that are used in the blog list components for indexing the blogs. There should be a typically looking RSS button (with icon) on the blog page. Blog index page should look creative and fun, e.g. not just a flat list but alternating left and right behavior with fun and engaging hover behavior e.g. exapnd card, exapand image, animate the border, animeate one hover colors. Be creative here.
- Projects reported on the web page should have avatars. Don't report Underflow as a project. Include select open source repos that I have created as projects and the YouTube channel as a project.
- There should also be social links with the appropriate svg icons.
  - X: https://x.com/jadengis/
  - GitHub: https://github.com/jadengis/
  - LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/john-dengis/
  - YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/@JohnDengisYT/
- For color inspiration, John likes black and red a lot with accents of white. Bright chromatic gradients are nice and a splash of color always makes things look good.
- Blog posts should be written in markdown and should contain jsonld content for better SEO indexing characteristics.
- Generated sitemap.xml
- Simple site map in the footer.
- Website should have good SEO and GEO practices built in.
- DO NOT copy the whole CV to the web page.
- Don't make to many references to Underflow in the content as I'm leaving the company.

## Notes

- Be creative in design. This isn't a SaaS app, or a landing page. It's a personal site for John Dengis and he is a creative person (to a fault). Animations, colors, and gradients are important ways of expressing these details.
- I can try to provide images where appropriate. Use placeholders as required and notify me of anything that needs to change.
